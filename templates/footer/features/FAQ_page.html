FAQ's

What is PVMP?

PVMP is a marketplace for PVAMU students. Users are able to sell and resell items that they own to other students on campus.

Is PVMP only for students?

Yes. PVMP was created soely for students on Prairie View's campus. When creating an account you are only able to provide the domain @pvamu.edu, which only pvamu students have.

How do I sell an item?

Users must send their item info (product name, category, images, and price) to the admin email account (a@a.com). Then the admin will accept or deny the request and post the item.

How do I create an account?

Navigate to the Login page. Click the link that says "Create an account" and fill out the requested information.

How do I delete my account?

Once logged in, navigate to your account dashboard. Click Login & Security. at the bottom of the page a red button that says "Delete your Account" is present. Click that button.

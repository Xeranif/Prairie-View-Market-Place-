FAQ's

What is PVMP?

PVMP is a marketplace for PVAMU students. Users are able to sell and resell items that they own to other students on campus.

Is this only for PVAMU students?

Yes. PVMP was created for the students of PVAMU.

Can anyone sell items?

Anyone with an account can post and sell items.
